Poway Women’s League

We have a league in Poway with 3 seasons a year.

3 Teams

  • Trash Bandits
  • Goalden Girls
  • Sirens

You play 2 weeks on and one week off, but are welcome to sub in for another team so you can still play every week.

All levels and age ranges (18+). Requires USA hockey membership and HECC gear.

UTC Pick Up Hockey & Clinic

We have women’s pick up hockey and clinic at the UTC ice rink

Pick up hockey

  • 2 teams (light vs dark)
  • Thursday’s at 9:20pm
  • $35 to play or $80 a month for unlimited games

Skills Clinic

  • Friday’s at 6:20pm
  • $25 per player

All levels and age ranges (18+). Requires USA hockey membership and HECC gear.
